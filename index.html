Our Story

From our family to yours, House of Taj provides its guest with the complete culinary experiance when it comes to enjoying traditional Indian cuisine. Our food consists of a vast array of spicy and sweet dishes, served in small size portions to allow the patron to sample and share a sizeable variety of items.

Our menu has it all; we try and satisfy all your cravings with all our delectable options: biryani, great Indian thali, vegetarian dishes, drinks, and desserts. Our immense variety of Indian food in one thali, flavorful biryani, and our savory desserts are just a few eamples of how we try to give you teh full authentic Indian cuisine experience
